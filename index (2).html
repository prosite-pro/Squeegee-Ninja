Our Story

CLEANING
WITH CRAFT.

Squeegee Ninja was founded by Joel Zephrine on one obsession: making glass disappear. When your windows are perfectly clean, you forget they're even there — that's the standard we hold ourselves to on every single job.

What started as a window cleaning operation in Philadelphia has grown into a full-service cleaning company trusted by hundreds of homeowners and businesses across the city and surrounding areas. Joel still shows up on jobs personally — because quality isn't something you outsource.
